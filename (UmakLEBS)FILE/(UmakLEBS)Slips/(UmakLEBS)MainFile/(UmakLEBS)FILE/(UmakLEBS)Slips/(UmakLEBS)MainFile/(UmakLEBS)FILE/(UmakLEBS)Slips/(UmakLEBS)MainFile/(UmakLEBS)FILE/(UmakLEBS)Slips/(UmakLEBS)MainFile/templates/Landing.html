About UMAK-LEBS

The University of Makati Laboratory Equipment Borrowing System (UMak-LEBS) is a comprehensive digital platform designed to streamline the management and tracking of laboratory equipment across all university departments.

Utilizing advanced RFID technology and digital management systems, UMak-LEBS ensures accurate inventory tracking, efficient equipment allocation, and seamless borrowing processes for students, faculty, and staff.

This innovative system enhances laboratory operations by providing real-time equipment availability, automated return reminders, and comprehensive usage analytics, ultimately supporting the university's commitment to academic excellence and research advancement.

Key Features

Discover the powerful capabilities that make UMak-LEBS the perfect solution for laboratory equipment management

RFID Technology

Advanced RFID tracking for real-time equipment monitoring and automated check-in/out processes with instant updates.

Automated Record Management

Keep borrowing logs organized and updated automatically for easy reference.

Real-Time Availability

View which items are available or borrowed instantly from the dashboard.

Self-Service Borrowing

Allows students and faculty to borrow and return laboratory tools through a simple kiosk or web interface without needing staff assistance.
